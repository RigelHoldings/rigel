What is a monthly charge?

Monthly Charge means the amount of Charges paid for the first full calendar month of the relevant Service giving rise to the claim.




Why do you Need to Pay?

These charges are for the Services that we provide to you in the Backend.
These Services Include Packaging costs of the Products. And the cost of the Manpower Used in Processing of the Orders.
And these Charges Also include the Cost of the Marketing and Expenditure on the File Storing, Managing GST Billings and The Inventory Charges.




From when these Charges will start to Apply?

All of the Monthly Charges will start to Apply when the overall Sales on Client's id cross the Threshold of ₹15,000 in the cycle of 30 Days.




How Much do you need to Pay?

All of the Above Mention services will cost you only ₹1,000 for 30 Days.
In case of Platinum PRIME membership the Charges will increase to ₹5,000 Due to the Additional Cost of the Renting of the Property For GST Registration.
